Iced Water Recipe

Description:

Iced water is a classic beverage for quenching thirst. Stuck with warm water? I'll teach you how to make it cold!

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Ice
  • Lemon (optional)

Steps:

  1. Pour water into a glass
  2. Add ice to water
  3. Optional: Add a lemon for extra electrolytes!
