Iced Water Recipe

Description:

Warm Milk is the classic treat we are told helps us fall asleep! Today I will teach you how to make it.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef Patty
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Whatever toppings you want!

Steps:

  1. Season the patty on both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  2. Heat up the Grill until the internal temperature is 450 degrees fahrenheit
  3. Cook the patty on the grill for four minutes on each side, put the cheese on top of the burger with one minute remaining to make it gooey.
  4. Put the cooked patty on a bottom bun
  5. Then, just put whatever toppings you want on like:
    • Cheese
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Pickle
